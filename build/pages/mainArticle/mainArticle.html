about us

Fenix Repower has a team located in Norway and Ukraine with skills in renewable project development and delivery.

Our core values are honesty, integrity, and respect for people. These values are ingrained in our daily practices, client deliveries, and how we treat our staff and external stakeholders.

The company was established as a consultancy in 2016 by senior executives from Royal Dutch Shell and has grown into predominantly working with renewables. ​

The Fenix Repower Code of Conduct is in full compliance with the Norwegian, EU and Ukrainian legal frameworks. We have strong experience in providing transparency and legal compliance while delivering projects timely.

Fenix Repower is an equal-opportunity employer, and we recognize our role in promoting positive corporate stewardship and sustainable business practices.
